👕 What to pack & wear
  • Light, breathable fabrics — cotton & linen. Loose-fitting clothes beat the humidity.
  • Compact umbrella ×2 — one for the diaper bag, one spare. Essential in both cities.
  • Light rain jacket / packable poncho each + stroller rain cover for Zainab.
  • A light layer/cardigan — malls, MRT/MTR & restaurants are aggressively air-conditioned.
  • Quick-dry clothes & sandals — you'll get caught in showers; fast-drying helps.
  • Sun protection — hats, sunscreen SPF 50+, sunglasses. UV is strong between showers.
  • Waterproof bag / dry pouch for phone, passports & electronics on wet days.
  • Swimwear — hotel pools & Sentosa; sea is a warm ~29°C.
